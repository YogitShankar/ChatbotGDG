344A - MagnetsBy the definition each block consists of a number of consequent and equally oriented dominoes. That means that in places where adjacent dominoes are not oriented equally, one block ends and another block starts. So, if there are x such places, the answer is equal to x + 1.Solution complexity: O(n).
